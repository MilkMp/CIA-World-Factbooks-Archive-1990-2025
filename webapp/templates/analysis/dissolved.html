Methodology
Tracks successor states of three dissolved federations: the Soviet Union (dissolved 1991, 15 successors), Yugoslavia (dissolved 1991, 6 successors), and Czechoslovakia (dissolved 1992, 2 successors). Pre-dissolution data is stored under the parent state’s ISO code in the archive. Values are parsed from CIA World Factbook text fields using standardized extraction patterns.
